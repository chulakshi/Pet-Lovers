Useful To Know

Cats

Cats are small, furry mammals with sharp claws and a playful, independent nature.They are one of
the most popular pets in the world, known for their agility, hunting skills, and affectionate personalities. Cats come in a variety of colors and patterns, and can have short or long hair. They have a keen sense of smell and excellent night vision, making them skilled hunters of small prey. They are also known to be independent, but they can also form strong bonds with their human caregivers.

Dogs

Dogs are intelligent, loyal and loving mammals. They are known to be one of the most popular pets in the world,
known for their ability to form strong bonds with their human caregivers.Dogs come in a wide variety of breeds, each with their own unique characteristics and personalities. They can be small lap dogs or large working breeds. They have a keen sense of smell and hearing which makes them great companions for hunting, herding, and as service animals.

Hamster

Hamsters are small, cute, and active rodents that make great pets. They are known for their playful and curious nature and are
. popular among both children and adults. They are low maintenance pets that are easy to take care of, and are known for their small size and portability. They come in a variety of colors and can have long or short hair. They are nocturnal animals, meaning they are most active at night and sleep during the day.

Birds

Lovebirds are small, brightly colored parrots that are known for their affectionate nature and
strong bonds with their mates.They are popular pets due to their playful and active personalities, and their ability to form strong bonds with their human caregivers. They come in a variety of colors, such as green, blue, and yellow, and have a distinctive head feather pattern. They are known to be social animals, and they should be kept in pairs or groups, as they thrive on interaction with their own kind.

Fish

Fish are available in many different varieties such as fresh water, saltwater, and tropical fish.
Freshwater fish come in a wide variety of colors, patterns, and sizes and can be easy to care for. Saltwater fish require a more advanced setup with specialized equipment and a more strict maintenance schedule.Tropical fish are known for their bright colors and exotic appearance and can be a great addition to a home aquarium.

Sugar Glider

Sugar gliders are small, marsupial mammals that are known for their ability to glide through the air using a membrane of skin
called a "patagium" that stretches between their front and hind legs.They are known for their distinctive, large eyes, and their soft, furry bodies. They are social animals and should be kept in pairs or small groups. They are nocturnal animals and are active at night, they have a lifespan of around 10-15 years and they require a large cage with plenty of room to move around and play.
